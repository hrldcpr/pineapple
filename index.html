My background.

I teach yoga and qi gong because I care about people. I care about bodies. I believe caring for ourselves and for each other is where the mundane and sacred meet.

I started practicing yoga in 2012 shortly after I moved to New York. As I passed through the worlds of academia, education, and nonprofits, my practice rooted and oriented me. When I found my way to qi gong in 2017, I knew exploring embodied consciousness would be the root of my life’s work.

In 2018, I started to teach. It has been an honor to work sharing the practices that have transformed my life. I can’t promise they will be transformative for you, but I hope you will explore their potential..
